YOGiFY - Find your yoga flow

Roles : UX/UI Designer

Duration : 4 Weeks

Overview

Yoga, combines physical postures, breath control, and meditation for holistic well-being. Beyond enhancing flexibility and strength, it fosters mental clarity and emotional balance. However, a notable barrier exists for yoga enthusiasts – the lack of knowledge regarding proper sequences and guidance. This gap can impede individuals from fully experiencing the stress-reducing and transformative benefits of yoga.

Problem

  • People find it difficult to do yoga without guidance.
  • It's hard to find a personalised yoga tutorial.
  • It's easy to get bored with the same tutorial.

Proposed Solutions

  • A personalised yoga sequence based on your level.
  • Users can review and customise the sequence.

"

Yoga enthusiasts do Yoga regularly in order to release stress. However, lack of knowledge in creating personalised routines and difficulty in determining the correctness of poses is not only making them lack motivation, but is making them more stressed.

"

Research

My research initially focused on conducting one-on-one interviews. This approach enabled me to understand the step-by-step process of practicing yoga and identify specific issues that could cause discomfort. Secondly, I observed the competitive landscape to understand how other yoga apps are addressing similar concerns.

What I found

  • Users do yoga for the physical and spiritual benefits.
  • Lack of visual guidance, led to loss of motivation.
  • If they were doing yoga at home, especially beginners, they wouldn't know alignment as well.
